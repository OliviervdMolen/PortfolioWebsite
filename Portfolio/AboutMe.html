YOUNG INTERESTS

For as long as I have known I have liked technology. From playing simple games to developing my own programs, most of my time went into computers.

The passion for technology started with my first laptop. I got a game for my birthday and could not stop playing it.

This passion only grew after time. When my first gaming-desktop arrived, my interests also changed to creating software myself.
By the time I finished highschool, Artificial-Intelligence came onto my radar. The software as well as the psychological part of neural networks seemed fascinating.
Now I am in college, studying OPEN-ICT and focussing on the subjects where my interests lay. HIGH SCHOOL ENDING ESSAY At the end of my high school years, I was required to write an essay about one of my interests. The subject I chose was the influence of technology on stock-trading.
